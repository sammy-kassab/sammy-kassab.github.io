About Page

Welcome to my website that is dedicated to helping undergraduates at the University of Berkeley studying mathematics. When I first started CAL, I had to sort through tons of data bases to even find something remotely helpful, so I created this site to hopefully be a math major's one stop shop.

The first thing I will reccommend is that you learn how to code... even if its just a little bit. Any sort of calculus based class where they require you to solve f(x) multiple times can be done in an instant using a simple language like python. If you are a math major, I will push that you learn MATLAB because you will need to know how to use it for upper division courses. Under the "code" tab, I have attached a bunch of codes that my professors have used in the past.

I do plan on adding more codes and tests to the site, but if you do want to help out I will be happy add whatever code/test you send me to the site.

I can also tutor... which was the main purpose of the about page. My name is Sammy Kassab, Class of 2020. If you would like to contact me scroll up and use the contact button!
